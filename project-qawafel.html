The Challenge

Traditional wholesale commerce in the Middle East relies on fragmented processes—phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and manual coordination. Qawafel aims to digitize this entire ecosystem, connecting vendors and businesses through a unified platform.

Product Discovery

The platform features a comprehensive product catalog with detailed specifications, pricing, and vendor information. Businesses can browse thousands of products with advanced filtering and search capabilities.

Featured Content

The homepage highlights hot deals, trending products, and featured suppliers to help businesses discover the best wholesale opportunities quickly.

Bilingual Experience

Designed for the Middle Eastern market, Qawafel provides seamless Arabic and English interfaces with proper RTL support, ensuring accessibility for all users in the region.
