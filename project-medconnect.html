MedConnect Platform

End-to-end redesign of a healthcare scheduling platform, improving workflow efficiency by 40% and reducing user errors

Role Lead Product Designer
Timeline 6 months (2024)
Team 2 Designers, 4 Engineers
Platform Web Application

Overview

MedConnect is a healthcare scheduling platform used by medical professionals to coordinate patient appointments, manage resources, and streamline workflows. The existing system was outdated, confusing, and led to frequent scheduling errors that impacted patient care.

Our goal was to redesign the entire platform with a focus on usability, reducing cognitive load, and creating a modern interface that healthcare workers could learn quickly and use confidently.

The Challenge

Complex Workflows

Healthcare scheduling involves multiple variables: doctor availability, room allocation, equipment needs, and patient preferences.

High Error Rate

The old interface led to 15% scheduling errors, causing delays and frustration for both staff and patients.

Poor Usability

Staff required extensive training and frequently forgot how to use key features.

The Solution

We conducted extensive user research, interviewed 25+ healthcare professionals, and observed their workflows to understand pain points. Based on insights, we redesigned the platform with:

  • Intuitive drag-and-drop scheduling interface
  • Smart conflict detection and resolution
  • Clear visual hierarchy and information architecture
  • Contextual help and onboarding
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go access

Key Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of designing for real-world complexity. Healthcare workflows are nuanced, and solutions need to be flexible yet structured. Working closely with end users throughout the process was critical to creating a tool they truly loved.

One key insight: sometimes the best design is invisible. By removing unnecessary UI elements and focusing on clarity, we helped users complete tasks without thinking about the interface at all.
